How to upload reviews (3 outlets)

  1. Log into each outlet's Google account
  2. Go to takeout.google.com
  3. Click "Deselect all", check only "Google Business Profile"
  4. Click "Next step" then "Create export"
  5. Repeat for all 3 outlets (Jurong, Pasir Ris, Bishan)
  6. Select all 3 ZIPs and drop them here at once
