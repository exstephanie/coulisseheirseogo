How to upload reviews

  1. Log into the brand's Google account
  2. Go to takeout.google.com
  3. Click "Deselect all", check only "Google Business Profile"
  4. Click "Next step" then "Create export"
  5. Wait for the email from Google, download the ZIP
  6. Drop the ZIP here (any file size works)
