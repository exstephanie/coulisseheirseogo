At Coulisse Heir, your [Reset 45](https://coulisseheir.com/treatment/reset-45/) journey begins with understanding your unique wellness rhythm. Knowing when to return for your next session, though, can feel overwhelming.

## Your Scalp's Natural Reset Cycle: Understanding the Foundation

Your scalp operates on a 28-day cellular renewal cycle, similar to facial skin. Environmental factors in Singapore accelerate this process significantly.

The humid climate causes increased sebum production and bacterial growth. Most clients benefit from more frequent treatments than those in temperate climates.

Our Reset 45 treatment works with your natural cycle to restore balance. The deep cleansing process removes buildup that accumulates over weeks of exposure to pollution and humidity.

Most scalp microbiome research suggests imbalances respond best to consistent, spaced-out treatment — typically every two to four weeks.

### Climate Impact on Scalp Health

Singapore's year-round humidity affects everyone differently. Most people experience increased oiliness and sensitivity during particularly humid months.

The constant air conditioning transitions also stress your scalp's natural barrier function, making regular professional treatments essential rather than optional.

## Reading Your Scalp's Signals: When It's Time to Return

Your scalp communicates its needs through various signals. Learning to recognise these indicators ensures optimal timing for your next ION Orchard scalp treatment.

### Physical Indicators

Increased itchiness typically appears first, usually around day 14-21 after treatment. You might also notice flakiness returning or hair feeling heavier at the roots.

Tenderness when touching your scalp indicates inflammation building up. Decreased hair volume suggests product buildup affecting your follicles.

Oil production changes also signal treatment timing. If your hair becomes greasy within 24 hours of washing, your scalp needs professional attention.

### Emotional and Lifestyle Factors

Stress directly impacts scalp health through cortisol production. During high-pressure periods, consider booking our [SHE Calm ritual](https://coulisseheir.com/treatment/she-calm/) for comprehensive restoration.

Travel frequently disrupts your scalp's balance due to climate changes and product variations. Post-travel treatments help reset your scalp's equilibrium.

## Building Your Personal Reset 45 Ritual: Frequency Guidelines for ION Orchard Scalp Treatment

Individual needs vary significantly based on hair type, lifestyle, and scalp condition. Our experience with hundreds of clients reveals clear patterns.

### Maintenance Phase (Healthy Scalps)

Most clients achieve optimal results with Reset 45 sessions every 3-4 weeks. This frequency maintains the benefits while working with your natural renewal cycle.

Active individuals or those with oily scalps may prefer every 2-3 weeks. Those with sensitive or dry scalps often find 4-5 weeks ideal.

The key lies in consistency rather than frequency. Regular treatments compound their benefits, creating lasting improvements in scalp health.

Seasonal adjustments also prove beneficial. During Singapore's particularly humid months, many clients increase frequency slightly.

Life events may require schedule adjustments. Wedding preparations, job changes, or health issues can all impact your ideal frequency.

Track how your scalp feels between sessions. This awareness helps you recognise when adjustments might benefit your routine.

## Maintaining Stillness Between Sessions: At-Home Scalp Care

Professional treatments provide the foundation, but daily care extends their benefits. Your at-home routine significantly impacts how long treatment effects last.

### Daily Rituals for Scalp Wellness

Gentle scalp massage during shampooing stimulates circulation without irritation. Avoid aggressive scrubbing that can damage your scalp's protective barrier.

Choose sulfate-free products that won't strip away the beneficial oils restored during your ION Orchard scalp treatment. Lukewarm water also prevents excessive drying.

Proper pillow hygiene often gets overlooked. Change pillowcases every 2-3 days to prevent bacterial buildup that can undo professional treatment benefits.

### Weekly Enhancement Practices

Dedicated scalp massage sessions once weekly help maintain circulation improvements. Spend 5-10 minutes using gentle circular motions.

Dry brushing before washing distributes natural oils and removes dead skin cells. This practice prepares your scalp for deeper cleansing.

Stress management directly impacts scalp health. Incorporating meditation or breathing exercises supports your wellness journey.

### Environmental Protection Strategies

Singapore's pollution requires protective measures between treatments. Covering hair during heavy traffic exposure helps minimise particulate buildup.

Air conditioning can over-dry your scalp. Use a humidifier in your bedroom to maintain optimal moisture levels overnight.

Swimming requires special attention due to chlorine exposure. Rinse immediately after swimming and use clarifying treatments as needed.
